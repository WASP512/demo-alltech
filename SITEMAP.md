# AllTech Website — Site Map

A plain-language map of every page on the site and how visitors move through it.
Intended as a quick reference for stakeholders — no technical background required.

---

## At a glance

- **Primary call to action:** the free **Security Gap Assessment** (a short quiz that scores a visitor's security posture and sends the result to our sales team).
- **Secondary call to action:** **Contact** / phone.
- **Roughly 30 pages**, including 11 local-area pages that help us show up in nearby searches.

---

## Where we send people (conversion paths)

1. **Home → Security Gap Assessment → Sales.**
   The hero button and a dedicated home section both drive to the assessment;
   completed results land in the sales inbox with the prospect's contact details.

2. **Any page → Contact.**
   The "Get in touch" button is always in the top-right; the contact form routes
   to our team inbox.

3. **Services → individual service pages → Contact / Assessment.**
   Each service explains what we do and points back to a conversion step.
